Founding Team

Raven Partners was founded by three senior investment bankers from Arma Partners, bringing decades of combined experience advising technology companies on M&A and growth capital transactions.

Simon Carmichael
Managing Partner
Simon has over 20 years of investment banking experience advising high-growth internet and technology businesses. Prior to founding Raven Partners, he was Partner and Co-Head of Private Capital at Arma Partners. Earlier, he was Managing Director at Torch Partners, advising companies including Just-Eat, Secret Escapes, and Notonthehighstreet.com. He started his career at Hambrecht & Quist and CSFB Technology Group. Simon holds an M.Eng in Metallurgy from Oxford University.
